About me

🇿🇦 Graphic designer and aspiring front-end developer, based in South Africa 💜







As a 23-year-old professional graphic designer with a growing interest in front-end development, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical skill to my work. Being born and raised in the diverse landscape of South Africa, I find inspiration in the rich tapestry of cultures that make up my home.

While I cherish moments of solitude, my passion for connecting with others through my designs is unwavering. Cooking, scrapbooking, and losing myself in the rhythm of music are some of the simple joys that color my days.

An introvert by nature, I embrace my preference for quiet introspection, finding solace in the sanctuary of my thoughts. Yet, this does not deter my enthusiasm for lifelong learning; each day presents an opportunity to expand my horizons and deepen my understanding of the world.

Despite my affinity for all things feminine-from makeup to fashion=I surprise many with my love for mobile shooting games, a hobby I happily share with my nephews. It's this contrast of interests that defines my multifaceted identity, reminding me that there's beauty in embracing the unexpected.
